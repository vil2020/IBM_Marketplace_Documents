FAQs

What is visualised Data?

  • Visualise data displays information, technical, Operation, factual, static and dynamic information in both pictorial and textual forms, using dev9ce accepted media, in situ, in context and overlaid using Georeferenced metadata.
  • Visualised data matures as its contributors change to the working and operational environment. So a fully integrated data set to 3rd Party sources and easily accessed and managed using common touch screen techniques and user gestures

Visualised Data is textual information presented in photographically in 360o panoramas, in context and in location.

How can I get Started?

VI consult with you to gather information about use cases, business challenges and pain points.

A plan to build and configure the solution is compiled and agreed.

Can VI help me complete build a complete 3D visualisation of my facility and it's assets.

  • Yes, VI offer a complete service from cradle to grave,
  • Firstly, VI will capture a Lidar image of your facility, using state of the art cameras and scanners.
  • The scan is processed, configured and published.
  • A Site Model is derived defining the Walls, doors, steps, lifts etc
  • A Navigation graph is built defining usable routes through the facility.
  • Assets are Located and tagged

Is my 3D scan integrated to MAS 9.x?

Yes. The VI App is fully integrated to Maximo 7.6.1.x and all version of MAS.

  • VI support both Red-Hat Openshift and Red0hat Single Node Openshift architectures.

How is my Facility captured?

  • VI undertake the capture of the facility using Lidar (Laser based cameras and also 4K cameras for photogrammetry.) The specialist equipment is carried around by our team of master technicians who ensure that all areas are covered in the scan and the photogrammetry.
  • Where accuracy of measurements is important, then SLAM anchors can be used

How often should my scan be updated?

  • This depends on how often the structure of the facility changes or the internal usage is redefined. Typically, twice a year is common for most facilities.

Can I produce my own Lidar scan?

  • Reality Capture can be undertaken by anyone who has the required equipment and has the knowledge / access to the services to process, configure and publish the results.

How long does it take to get up and running?

  • VI can scan between 10,000 and 30,000 SqM per day, depending upon the complexity of the site being scanned.
  • for each 10,000SqM, we need approx 3 day to process and publish the scan.
  • AT this stage, then visual is ready to be used.
  • Configuration of the scan with the VI App is straight forward and scripted, and can be completed in a matter of days when the use cases have been agreed.

Is a Fully Managed Service Available

How do I capture IoT data

  • The VI IoTHub is a feature rich tool that listens to sensors of all types, makes and protocols. It ingests the data, make sense of it and presents it for consumption.

Can I visualise IoT Data

  • The VI InfoHub is a comprehensive suite of widget based tools for the display of data, including IoT, Assets, Work Orders and even the weather form the IBM weather channel.

Will my IoT Data be accessible externally?

  • A REST API is provided for access to your data held within the VI Database. Data formats supported include JSON, XML and CSV.
  • The InfoHub offers an export feature for each Widget into Excel.

Is my IoT data integrated into Maximo Meters?

  • Yes. VI work with you to ensure that all Maximo Meters are mapped to the IoT Device Types in use within your facility. A logical map is built between the scan and Maximo for use by the API's to move data between Maximo Meters and the 3D View.
